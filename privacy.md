# Ollama Sidebar – Privacy Policy

*Last updated: June 2026*

## What data is collected

None, by the extension itself. When you use the "Ask about this page" feature, the text content of your current browser tab is sent only to the Ollama instance running on your own machine (or another host you've explicitly configured in Settings) to generate a response. This data never leaves your local network unless you've pointed the extension at a remote Ollama host yourself.

## What data is stored locally

Your Ollama host setting, selected model, and conversation history are stored in your browser's local extension storage (`chrome.storage.local`). This data never leaves your device.

## What data the developer collects

None. The extension has no backend server. The developer does not receive, store, or process any user data.

## Third parties

None. The extension communicates only with the Ollama host you configure (`http://localhost:11434` by default). No analytics, tracking, or advertising services are used.
